# Vision — Sales OS

## Mission

Become the **easiest AI Sales OS** for SMB companies in Russia and CIS.

## North Star

```
Manager receives a call
        ↓
AI records it
        ↓
AI fills CRM
        ↓
AI creates tasks
        ↓
Manager spends zero time on CRM administration
```

**Everything else is secondary.**

## Who we serve

B2B sales teams (5–50 people) who outgrew spreadsheets but refuse Bitrix24 complexity.

## What success looks like (MVP)

First paying customer completes:

Register company → Invite team → Login → Create contact → Receive call → Get AI summary → Continue in CRM
